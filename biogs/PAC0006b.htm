Program In Search of Your Warrior (1995 - )

From
1995
Canada
Website
http://www.csc-scc.gc.ca/publications/lt-en/2005/30-4/3-eng.shtml

Summary

A program established to assist Aboriginal people who struggle with violent behaviour to understand the cycle of violence, and how historic trauma, personal trauma, childhood experiences and grief and loss have affected their behaviour. The process is founded upon ceremony and teachings provided by traditional Elders and could not exist without ceremony, Elders' guidance, and the teaching of natural law. Beginning in 1995 at the Stan Daniels Healing Centre, the program expanded in 1999 to include services for men in federal correctional institutions. In 2002 the program expanded yet again to include women in federal correctional institutions. In 2009 a young offenders program was initiated.
